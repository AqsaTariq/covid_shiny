Several countries have taken drastic measures to combat coronavirus.
China was the first, sealing off the city of Wuhan at the end of January.
A distinction can be made between two different types of lockdown.
Complete and partial lockdown.
A lockdown is an emergency protocol that prevents people from leaving a particular area.
A full lockdown means that you must stay where you are and that you are not allowed to leave or enter a building or the given area.
This scenario usually allows important supplies, grocery stores, pharmacies and banks to continue serving people.
All non-essential activities will remain closed during the whole period.
During a partial lockdown, some things may be allowed.
For example, gyms and restaurants may remain open or gatherings of more than two people may be permitted.
A lockdown can apply to a whole country or parts of it.
