November 17, 2019
On Nov. 17, the first case of the novel coronavirus occurred in the Hubei province in China.
Not much is known about patient 0 except that he is a 55-year-old man from Wuhan.
The city that will soon be known as the epicenter of the coronavirus outbreak.
